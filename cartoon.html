The Inventables

In a near post-apocalyptic suburb, you and your friends must invent ways to stay alive,

while solving puzzles and fulfilling quests for bizarre townies. A "Falloutlike" game

meant for a T for Teen audience, the scope of crafting and muzzles in this game is the

size of your imagination.
